# NEURAL LINK · Trading Session Discipline Terminal

> *The outside world ends when the session begins.*

**Live App →** https://farman024.github.io/Neural-Link/

---

## What It Is

Neural Link is a cyberpunk-themed discipline dashboard built for serious traders. When the London or New York session opens, you launch Neural Link and lock in. The app tracks your presence, detects when you drift away, and pulls you back before a distraction becomes a bad trade.

Most traders don't lose because of bad strategy. They lose because they check Instagram mid-session. Neural Link exists to stop that.

---

## Features

- **Session Timer** — Tracks London Session (8:00–17:00 GMT) and New York Session (13:00–22:00 GMT) in real time
- **Tab Drift Detection** — If you switch tabs or leave the app on mobile, Neural Link detects it and fires a warning
- **Lock-In Counter** — Tracks how many times you drifted per session — your discipline score
- **Cyberpunk UI** — Dark terminal aesthetic built for focus, not decoration
- **PWA** — Installable on mobile for full-screen session mode
- **Supabase Sync** — Session stats persist across devices

---

## The Philosophy

A trading session is a professional obligation. Neural Link treats it like one. No motivational quotes. No gentle nudges. Just a system that holds you accountable to the session you committed to.
